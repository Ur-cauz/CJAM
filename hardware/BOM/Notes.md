## Purchasing Notes

- Use genuine or high-quality NRF24L01+ modules where possible, as very cheap clones may have reduced RF performance.

- The 100µF capacitors are strongly recommended for each NRF24L01+ module to improve power stability.

- Standard 2.54mm headers are used throughout the design.

- The PCB is designed around the ESP32 DevKit V1 (38-pin) form factor. Other ESP32 boards may not fit without modifications.


This BOM should be complete for assembling a CJAM Rev A board. Once your prototype arrives and you've verified everything works, you can update it with exact manufacturer part numbers (e.g., specific OLED module, capacitor dimensions, etc.) for a more production-ready BOM.
