# FAQ

## Why three NRF24 modules?

To allow multiple independent radios on a single ESP32.

---

## Why use an ESP32?

Built-in Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, excellent performance, and wide community support.

---

## Why does each NRF have two capacitors?

100µF provides bulk current.

0.1µF filters high-frequency noise.

---

## Can I remove one NRF?

Yes.

Each module can operate independently.

---

## Is the enclosure required?

No.

The board works without it.
